Appendix Tab Content Redesign

Information Architecture

Three tabs have readability issues: "What Changed" (confusing name, overlaps with Consistency), "Iterations" (flat cards instead of a visual timeline), and "Consistency" (duplicates findings shown elsewhere). The question: how do we restructure the content within each tab to be scannable and non-redundant?
